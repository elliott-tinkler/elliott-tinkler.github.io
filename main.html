My Favorite Shows

My favorite shows include The Good Place, Schitt's Creek, Demon Slayer, The Boys, and INVINCIBLE. These shows and their messages have stuck with me and shaped who I am today. The Good Place introduced me to ethics. Schitt's Creek taught me to see in the shoes of others. Demon Slayer inspired an unshakable will in my heart, and lastly, The Boys and INVINCIBLE taught me that actions speak louder than words.
